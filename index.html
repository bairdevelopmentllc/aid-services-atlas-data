Find aid and services by location, even when you are offline.

Aid & Services Atlas (ASA) is an offline-first, location-aware directory that helps people discover public and charitable services by need and county-equivalent area. It is designed to be trustworthy, neutral, and privacy-first.

What ASA is

A public-interest directory that helps people find essential services by need and location.

  • Federal, state, and local data layers
  • Human-maintainable and validated data
  • Designed for broad accessibility

What ASA is not

  • Not emergency response or dispatch
  • Not a verified endorsement of any provider
  • Not a data-harvesting platform

How it works

ASA determines a general admin area (county-equivalent) and downloads the data needed for that area. Providers are combined from federal, state, and local layers at runtime.

1) Detect or select location

Uses coarse location and county-equivalent admin areas. If it cannot resolve, ASA falls back to the last known area.

2) Choose what you need

Select needs like food, housing, medical, transportation, and more.

3) Browse offline

Once downloaded, results remain accessible offline, with updates syncing silently when online.
